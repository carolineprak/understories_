## How It Works

Understories evaluates messages against 8 evidence-based criteria:

1. **Clarity** — Is the message immediately understandable?
2. **Emotional Resonance** — Does it connect emotionally without overwhelming?
3. **Actionability** — Is there a clear, specific, achievable action?
4. **Solution-Focus** — Does it emphasize pathways forward (not just problems)?
5. **Credibility** — Are claims believable and backed by evidence?
6. **Accessibility** — Is the language inclusive and jargon-free?
7. **Hope/Doom Balance** — Does it balance urgency with agency?
8. **Personal Relevance** — Does it connect to the audience's lived experience?

**Each criterion** draws from peer-reviewed research and includes:
- Scoring rubric (1-10 scale with rationale)
- Why it matters (research citations)
- What high/low scores look like
- Specific guidance for improvement

**The result:** 
Messages go from 5/10 to 9/10. 
Double the donations. Double the signatures. Campaigns that actually win.

## Multi-Framework Architecture
Understories isn't a single tool—it's an integrated system where four frameworks check each other:

### 1. Evidence Synthesis (8 Criteria)
50+ peer-reviewed reports distilled into actionable guidance. 
Emotional Resonance draws from narrative frameworks. Personal Relevance assesses psychological distance. 
Hope/Doom Balance evaluates narrative arc—moving from problem to agency to possibility.

### 2. Ethical Guardrails
6 principles across the AI lifecycle ensure non-maleficence, autonomy, and justice. 

**Key commitments:** 
AI suggests, humans decide. Citations for every recommendation. No optimization for manipulation.

### 3. Environmental Transparency
Carbon cost disclosure for every evaluation. 

**Impact:** 
~6,500 kg CO₂/year at scale — negligible compared to campaigns enabled.

### 4. Narrative Structure (Integrated)
Rather than teaching storytelling separately, Understories evaluates whether messages use narrative principles effectively—through Emotional Resonance (narrative arc), Personal Relevance (psychological distance), and Actionability (clear "Story of Now" moment).

**Why these frameworks check each other:**
- Evidence informs ethics → Research shows what works; ethics ensures responsible use
- Ethics validates storytelling → Narrative is powerful; ethics prevents manipulation
- Environmental impact ensures sustainability → We measure what we model
- Storytelling makes research human → Academic findings become actionable

**Most AI tools add ethics later. Understories designed these frameworks to check each other from day one.**
